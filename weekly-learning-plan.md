# 🧠 Learn AI – Weekly Learning Plan

This document outlines a weekly plan to complete the AI learning journey using foundational and advanced books.  
Each week assumes:  
- ✅ 1 hour of reading per day  
- ✅ 1 hour of coding practice per day  
- 🗓️ 5 study days per week

---

| Book                                | Duration |
|-------------------------------------|----------|
| Learning Python                     | 16 weeks |
| Data Science from Scratch           | 4 weeks  |
