How many calories to burn in a day for weight loss?

**Step 1: Determine the Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)**
Formula (Male): (10 x weight in kilograms) + (6.25 * height in centimetres) - (5 * age in years) + 5
Formula (Female): (10 x weight in kilograms) + (6.25 * height in centimetres) - (5 * age in years) - 161

**Step 2: Factor in activity level**

|     | Activity Level    | Active Calories per Day (approx.) |
| --- | ----------------- | --------------------------------- |
| 1   | Sedentary         | 0–200 kcal                        |
| 2   | Lightly Active    | 201–400 kcal                      |
| 3   | Moderately Active | 401–700 kcal                      |
| 4   | Very Active       | 701–1000 kcal                     |
| 5   | Super Active      | 1000+ kcal                        |

**Step 3: Calculate Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE)**
Formula: BMR * Activity Factor

**Step 4: Calculate daily calorie intake target for fat loss**
Recommended daily deficit of 500 calories can lead to safe weight loss of roughly 0.5 kg per week. This means your daily calorie intake target should be your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) minus 500 calories.

- 1 gram of protein = 4 calories
- 1 gram of fat = 9 calories
- 1 gram of carbohydrate = 4 calories
- 1 gram of body fat = 7.7 calories
