> [!caution]+ **Progression & Effort**
> - Increase weights by **5–10%** if you can complete all reps with good form  
> - Rest: **60–90 sec (main lifts), 30–45 sec (accessory/core)**  
> - Rate of Perceived Exertion: **7/10 (moderate-hard)**  
> - Stop immediately if sharp pain in knees or shoulders
