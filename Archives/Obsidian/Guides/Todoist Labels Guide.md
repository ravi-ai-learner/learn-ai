# Todoist Labeling Guide (Minimal 7-Label System)

Use this to assign consistent, minimal labels to tasks. Focus on **context** and **function**, not specific items.

---

## 🏠 `@home`
> Tasks related to maintaining or managing your home or living space.

**Examples**:
- Pay electricity bill  
- Talk to milk vendor (WhatsApp)  
- Organize bookshelf  
- Clean kitchen  
- Schedule appliance repair  

---

## 🚶‍♂️ `@errands`
> Physical, outside-the-home tasks.

**Examples**:
- Buy fruits from local store  
- Pick up parcel  
- Visit pharmacy  
- Drop off laundry  
- Return item to store  

---

## 🩺 `@health`
> Anything for your body’s maintenance, fitness, or medical wellbeing.

**Examples**:
- Workout  
- Take vitamins  
- Doctor appointment  
- Prepare weekly workout plan  
- Log weight/progress  

---

## 🛍️ `@shopping`
> All non-edible purchases — online or in-person.

**Examples**:
- Buy gym apparel  
- Order phone charger  
- Purchase notebook  
- Buy Band-Aids  
- Get protein shaker  

---

## 🍎 `@food`
> Anything related to eating, drinking, or preparing food.

**Examples**:
- Grocery list  
- Prep smoothie  
- Buy eggs  
- Plan meals  
- Order lunch  

---

## 💼 `@work`
> Focused, productive tasks related to work, study, or deep thinking.

**Examples**:
- Write report  
- Respond to client email  
- Schedule team meeting  
- Read research article  
- Review code  

---

## 🙋‍♂️ `@personal`
> Tasks for self-improvement, hobbies, reflection, or downtime.

**Examples**:
- Journal  
- Meditation  
- Plan monthly goals  
- Read book  
- Practice guitar  

---

## 🔁 Labeling Rules Summary

| If task is about...                  | Use label      |
|-------------------------------------|----------------|
| Managing the home or household      | `@home`        |
| Going outside to get something done | `@errands`     |
| Your body, medicine, or fitness     | `@health`      |
| Buying physical (non-food) items    | `@shopping`    |
| Food, groceries, or cooking         | `@food`        |
| Work, study, deep-focus tasks       | `@work`        |
| Self-care, hobbies, goals           | `@personal`    |

---

Keep it clean. Let context guide your choice, not the item itself.
