# 🧠 Learn AI – Weekly Learning Journey

This document outlines a simplified, book-based plan for learning AI in a structured way over ~1.5 years.

Each week assumes:  
- ✅ 1 hour of reading  
- ✅ 1 hour of hands-on coding  
- 🗓️ 5 study days per week
